Scroll to see the header react

This filler content exists only to make the sticky header scroll-shrink demonstrable inside a contained frame, so it doesn't hijack the gallery page's own scroll.

Northbeam brings your workflow, your team, and your assets into a single fast surface — built for people who ship every day, not once a quarter.

Keep scrolling within this frame to confirm the header stays pinned to the top while the rest of the content moves beneath it.

At roughly 8px of scroll offset the transition fires: the bar's contents condense via a transform-only scale (the layout box never changes, per the motion spec), a hairline bottom border fades in, and a resting shadow settles underneath — all in one 220ms ease-out transition.

Once you scroll back up past the threshold, the header returns to its resting state on the same transition.
